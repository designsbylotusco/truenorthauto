Our Story

A Shop Built on Trust, Not Transactions.

TrueNorth Auto wasn't built overnight. It was built one car at a time, one honest conversation at a time. When we opened our doors on Yonge Street, we had one goal: be the kind of shop that people actually feel good about bringing their car to.

We know how stressful car trouble can be. The last thing you need is a mechanic who talks over your head, pads your bill, or tells you everything needs replacing. That's not how we operate. We'll always tell you what your car actually needs — and what it can wait on.

Over the years, we've become a go-to for families all across North York and East York. Some of our customers have been bringing their cars to us for over a decade. That kind of loyalty is something we take seriously — and work hard every day to deserve.
